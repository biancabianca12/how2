List of Illustrations

by Susan Johanknecht

Working Note:

“List of”' is a digital work in progress which relates to the texts and concerns of Geomorphology (see this issue of New Writing for extracts). Nuclear waste is reprocessed into other forms (glass pellets, graphite rods) and then buried. It will be toxic for a duration of thousands of years, perhaps beyond future memory, when it could conceivably be dug up as treasure from the past.
