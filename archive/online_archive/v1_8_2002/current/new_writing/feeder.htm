Installation: “The Feeder” and Modern (Laundry) Production

Modern (Laundry) Production

“The Feeder” and Modern (Laundry) Production were shown at the Standpoint Gallery (London) in January 2002. The book to be handled was on a waist-high shelf giving some privacy of looking, but with the viewer/reader positioned on a stage-space. Near the book, an extended sequence of pages spilled down from a high shelf onto the floor. Beside this an industrial-looking monitor showed the continual, silent, acting-out of the calender feeder.
