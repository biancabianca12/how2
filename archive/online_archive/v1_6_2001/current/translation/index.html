Introductory Remarks about this section:

 

This section will feature contemporary poetry -- and accompanying poetics / essay / journal writing, when possible -- translated into English from other languages. If you are translating work, please propose your ideas to our on-going translation coordinator: Sawako Nakayasu, <Sawako_Nakayasu@brown.edu>

Featuring poetry translations by:

Sawako Nakayasu translating Ito Hiromi

Rosa Alcalá translating Lourdes Vázquez

Louis Armand & Clare Wallace translating Véronique Vassiliou
