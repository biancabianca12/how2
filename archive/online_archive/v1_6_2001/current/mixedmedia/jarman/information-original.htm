the following

is an accumulation of materials

gathered

during six weeks of

continuously being on the move

through northern Spain

and westwards

before heading down

into Portugal

and the last of summer

in the

South of Spain

and briefly

Morocco on foot

leaving home

at the ferry port

parked

anonymous

and then rapidly North

myself

my accomplice

and our

converted delivery van

remnants of moments

places

times

and thoughts

overheard / seen exchanges

recorded and stored

revived

online

and returned
