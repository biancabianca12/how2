Once she looked outside her mother's window and saw a body lying in the gutter. Perhaps she called an ambulance. Today it is unclear. But the next day walking outside she saw a used needle lying beside where the body had been. The body had been a man and he had lived. She never had thought about the Doctor that had treated the body until reading the Doctor who wrote. Sometimes this Doctor who wrote wrote about bullet wounds like the stars. And so she thought about the Doctor who might have treated the man in the gutter. But not much.

Mina Loy lived in Mexico during the first World War and the draft dodging expatriates were called slackers. Just like the movie. (Texas borders Mexico.) Mina's love sailed away in a boat, just a test, and was never seen again. (What would a parenthesis be in a movie?).
