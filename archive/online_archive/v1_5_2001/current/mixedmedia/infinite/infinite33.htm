When one feels unaccepted by the mainstream of one's chosen paths the choices are to forge one's own sense of the work needing to be done or to feel inadequate, alienated. The modernists decided they were geniuses. Now the idea of genius is suspect. Seventy years from now what discoveries of the workings of the body will uncover a misguided idea of today? This will happen but it won't break the trail of texts. Forging the new is knowing the old.

The new is disorderly and lacks, it maybe, all correlation with a ready body of listeners. 43

To associate the experience of reading with images, audio, text, and more is not to deny a medium's inherent properties, or to create a hierarchy with literature at the top, but to broaden the idea of what reading is. Reading as agency.

. . . an attitude of inquiry, a manner of listening, a mode of recognizing what is significant and proceeding from there to identify networks of significance. 44
