Shin Yu Pai is author of Unnecessary Roughness (xPress(ed)), Equivalence (La Alameda Press), and Ten Thousand Miles of Mountains and Rivers (Third Ear Books). Two projects, The Love Hotel Poems (Press Lorentz) and Works on Paper (Convivio Bookworks) are forthcoming in 2006. She has exhibited her visual work at Gallery 2, The Three Arts Club, and The McKinney Avenue Contemporary.
