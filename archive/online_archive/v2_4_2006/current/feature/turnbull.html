Chris Turnbull has had work appear in Open Letter, TADS, Dandelion, fusebox, Undercurrents, the anthology Companions & Horizons: An Anthology of Simon Fraser University Poetry, and the first and second issues of Ottawater. Excerpts from "Continua" are part of a longer sequence of poems. She lives in Kemptville, Ontario.
