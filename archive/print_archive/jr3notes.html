Joan Retallack

Joan Retallack is the author of Circumstantial Evidence (S.O.S. Press). Errata 5uite is forthcoming from Edge Books. Recent poetry has been in Aerial, o-blek, Abacus, and Generator. Her critical essays have appeared in numerous journals and magazines.

Working Notes, Joan Retallack:

The work in this series has been exploring after and alter rimages, images, ages . . . . their aftermath as they meet on the internal edges of the page. The language results from an interaction between intuitive and chance procedures.
