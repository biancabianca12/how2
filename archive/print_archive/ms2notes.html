Margy Sloan

Margy Sloan has published work recently in Re*Map and Big Allis. New work will be appearing in the next Talisman, and an Abacus is scheduled for early 1993.

Working Notes, Margy Sloan:

These sonnets are the eleventh and twelfth in a series of ten, planned to number twenty-seven in all. They originated in a reading of Descartes' Discourse on Method and led into a consideration of modes of exploration--walking through landscapes as well as using instruments and numbers.
