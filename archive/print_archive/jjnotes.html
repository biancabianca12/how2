Johanna Jordahl

 

Johanna Jordahl is a recent graduate from the writing program at San Francisco State. Her appearance as a character in Eileen Corder's recent production with the Poets Theatre in S.F. has led to her interest in reading as performance.

WORKING NOTES FROM JOHANNA JORDAHL:

The most difficult project is restraining critics' voices that comment from behind or above or below and to give the lead to play. Poems are play as music is played. I like adventure. The goal is to listen to the reading in the head and to play well.
