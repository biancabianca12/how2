Meredith Stricker

Meredith Stricker writes in Iowa City. Her performance collaboration with Elizabeth Lahey in New York City includes "The Poor Body" and "Tele-." A reading of H.D. is forthcoming in Ink. Karen Ganz is a painter teaching at the University of Washington where she also constructs books & circulates collaborative work.

WORKING NOTES, MEREDITH STRICKER:

These pages are from a gothic romance called The Queen Bee, by Edna Lee, sent to me in the mail by Karen Ganz, a painter living in Seattle. We draw & paint into a series of books, glue in scraps & pictures to find/erase our text. Reading as a way of writing, then. Speaking not exactly English--more like a bee hum--paint speaking & torn edge & white space speaking.
