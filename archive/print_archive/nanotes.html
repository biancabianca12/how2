Nuala Archer

Nuala Archer lives in Oklahoma where she teaches and edits the Midland Review and the Cimarron Review. Her book, From a Mobile Home, is being read by Gallery Press for possible publication in 1988.

WORKING NOTES, NUALA ARCHER:

"Super 8 Software Wanderings" is a long piece of subtitles from a movie in my head about growing up in Panama. Much to my delight (and confirming my anxiety) I find my initials in the gap between Pa and Ma. In this poem I continue to explore the places I have lived as palimpsest. The context of this excerpt is a long piece called "Attic Antics" in which the personalities of two female flaneurs develop. The roof of their attic, like a planetarium, opens to other worlds and together they turn the usual madness of an attic's hoardings into play and pleasure.
