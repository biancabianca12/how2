Julia Blumenreich

Julia Blumenreich is a poet, painter and teacher living in Philadelphia. This fall she will be teaching secondary school teachers how to teach contemporary poetry to their students. Poems and articles have appeared or are forthcoming in: Heresies, St. Marks Poetry Project Newsletter, Manhattan Poetry Review and Central Park.

WORKING NOTES: JULIA BLUMENREICH:

Language as (always) a process of exchange. These are part of a collaborative project with another poet, Gil Ott. The method has been simple, each writing one or two entries then passing the growing manuscript back to the other.
In one context I am audience to the other's writing, in the next my participation is translated into direct response, and so on; it is the circular which seems a more accurate description of what proceeds / what is worth putting pen to paper about. Worth = possible? =/= probable (linear).
