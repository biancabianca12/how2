Lodging

Are we the detours in these parts. The most
awkward motivates without limit; a
porcupine headed that-a-way.

The court insists upon indiscriminate
privilege; no alliterations she says.

How to avoid the trap. Does it interfere
sufficiently with a logical progression
of loosely constructed faults.

To arrange words for motion. Why
are the whorls and dividers still

circling. The only relevant statements. Choking
or lodging: a place to be out of
the weather and sleep. Subtle surgery

of an alliterative night. Can we then doze.
The possibility that she and I will meet.

 

Frances Jaffer
