Easter Woman

 


Whoso, whomever, who, whom, you whom

I substantiate

whose sufferings I attend.

The indentation of her ass on the bench that waits.
Distinguish what is female.The oily gloss on her hair, her feet.
This rolls rocks away.

Casts heart or fear

by ruddy grass.

Elizabeth Robinson
