Kathryn MacLeod

Kathryn MacLeod lives in Vancouver, B.C. Her book, How Two, was published by Tsunami Press in 1987. She is on the editorial board of Motel, and a member of the Kootenay School of Writing.

Working Notes, Kathryn MacLeod:

These two pieces are part of a much larger work still in process. The idea of working on a project over an extended period of time (in this case two years) interests me a great deal. I do not yet see an end to "Houseworks," although I find that the work, and my involvement with it, is constantly changing.
