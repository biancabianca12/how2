Poem #10

preposition as of description
into words=script
long tresses
a man's belt wraps
some multi-colored bobbins assuage
a table from a cable spool

latitude(lass)as other's arms
round out length to
perpendicular easy dimensions
R & R (you & I reading & ) or
have equal
grow up to trees

a piece of paper lies level
if or-- tosses until
stops transferred

 

Julia Blumenreich
