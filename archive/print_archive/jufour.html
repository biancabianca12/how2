Four Poems



Force

You walk
across
a knowing

that the man
if he follows

beneath
the tree

will feel
your hand

unmoving
in a room



Off

That taking
under cotton

in the cab
on the way

Every
downtown block

stripping
toward slowly

**(right column begins-however blurry and partially illegible**)

Jane Underwood
