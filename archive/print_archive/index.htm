ARCHIVES
MAY, 1983 - JANUARY, 1992

Between May, 1983 and January, 1992, two dozen print-on-paper issues of HOW(ever) were created. Below are links to hypertext versions of those texts. The pages have been prepared by a team of professional and university information and text specialists. While some elements of experimental writing do not always lend themselves to simple hypertext conversion, every effort has been made to represent the work as it originally appeared.
