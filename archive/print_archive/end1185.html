Frances Jaffer's new collection of poems, Alternate Endings, is the first book issued by HOW(ever) Press . It may be ordered by sending $6.00 plus $1.00 postage & handling to HOW(ever) Press, 554 Jersey Street, San Francisco CA 94114.

Beverly Dahlen's new book, A Reading ( 1 - 7) has just been published by Momo's Press, and can be ordered by sending $12.50 to Momo's Press, 45 Sheridan St., San Francisco, CA 94103. Postage and handling are included on prepaid orders.

With this issue of HOW(ever) our second series is complete. Subscriptions for Volume III, Nos. 1-4 are currently invited. Until December 15, you may subscribe at the old rate of $6.00 for individuals, $8.00 for libraries and institutions. After December 15, 1985, the rates will go up $1 to $7.00 for individuals and $9.00 for libraries and institutions. Single copies are not for sale, except on a limited local basis, nor are free sample copies available. Subscription checks should go to : HOW(ever) , c/o Jaffer, 871 Corbett, San Francisco, CA 9413 1. Original experimentalist poetry manuscripts, informal commentary on neglected modernist women writers, "postcards" alerting us to current or lost works of poetry will be seriously considered.
