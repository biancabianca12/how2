JULIA VOSE

Julia Vose's first collection of poems, Moved Out on the Inside, is from The Figures. Her work has appeared in APR and Room.

WORKING NOTES, JULIA VOSE:

"A Depth of Pink" was a small sketch of commitment four years ago. Then I began to dream it over and over again. Over time, its pieces bubbled up to the surface. Then I ordered them and re-ordered them. Versions and versions. I work intensely for short periods. Go away, come back, go away, come back.
