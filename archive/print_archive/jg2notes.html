Joanna Gunderson

Joanna Gunderson was born in 1932. She has written every day since 1957.

Working Notes, Joanna Gunderson:

The works are works of voices they are not heard in sequential time.
Wind is from a journal kept March 21, 1985-March 21, 1986--other things noted--the angle of light, flowers blooming fading, the moon, snatches of conversations heard.
Mrs. Mann is from a large play, Color, about the turning of white light into the colors of the spectrum. She is green. Her lines which occur throughout the play and interplay with as many as 70 other voices are brought together here.
