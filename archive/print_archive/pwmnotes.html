Phyllis Webb

Phyllis Webb's poetry has influenced innovative practice in Canadian literature over the past several decades. Her recent books include Wilson's Bowl (poems), 1980, Coach House, and The Vision Tree, Selected Poems (winner of the Governor General's Award for Poetry), 1982, Quadrant Editions, Montreal. Recent work may be seen in the summer '89 issue of Malahat Review. She lives in Victoria, B.C.
