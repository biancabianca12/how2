GilbertandGrape have made the pledge to drive from the south to the north of Norway in a 1967 Volvo Amazon with their lone ranger: a 20 kilo stuffed moose head in the back seat. Driving through urban and rural landscapes with the immense dedication and commitment to their quest -to return the lone ranger to the sunset in Nordkapp (elg I solnedgang) where the midnight sun never sets. A series of incidents, phone calls, recommendations, bad map reading and detours led us to this point. Detours in the form of a direction from the people we meet on our Lone Ranging Romance. A detour to order a hotdog, bake a cream cake or visit a sunset. GilbertandGrape invite you to draw a detour on their 3000 mile long journey to recreate the moose in sunset.
