Responding to Public Figures:
Guidelines for Submitting your own Photographs, Poems and Texts

Is there a figurative statue carrying a weapon in your town or city? Please send picture(s) (at a resolution of at least 72 dpi) to shufran.lauren@gmail.com with the subject heading "Public Figures archive." If you have written anything about the statue or know anything about its history, please send that along too. Your submissions will be posted at this site. If they do not appear in a reasonable amount of time, please contact the address above.

Copyright

By submitting photographs and text, you are confirming that all appropriate steps have been taken with regard to copyright. This means that photographs and text are either produced by you or that you have explicit permission from the copyright holder for use in HOW2. In the latter case, you must provide citations and acknowledgments.
